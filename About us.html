Our Story


CarbonTrack was founded by a group of passionate environmentalists and tech innovators who saw an urgent need for actionable tools in the battle against climate change. We realized that many people and organizations want to reduce their carbon footprints but struggle to measure and understand their impact. With our expertise in technology, sustainability, and data science, we set out to create a platform that offers real-time carbon tracking, personalized recommendations, and easy-to-implement solutions for reducing emissions. Whether you’re an individual looking to live more sustainably or a business striving to meet your corporate social responsibility goals, CarbonTrack is here to help you take control of your environmental impact.

What We Do


We offer a comprehensive suite of tools and services designed to make carbon footprint reduction easy and effective:

Carbon Footprint Calculator: Our intuitive calculator helps you assess your carbon emissions based on daily activities, travel, energy consumption, and more. Get an instant snapshot of your impact on the planet. Customized Reduction Plans: Based on your footprint, we provide personalized recommendations that guide you toward practical and impactful actions, from reducing waste to adopting renewable energy solutions.
Business Solutions: CarbonTrack also offers tailored tools for businesses to monitor, track, and reduce their carbon footprint. Our platform provides insights on how to optimize supply chains, energy use, and more to help organizations achieve sustainability goals.
Real-Time Tracking: Our app and dashboard offer live tracking of your progress as you take steps to lower your carbon footprint, helping you stay motivated and on track. Educational Resources: Stay informed with our collection of articles, case studies, webinars, and more, offering valuable insights on sustainability, carbon reduction strategies, and the latest environmental trends.
